    About Us

Welcome to our Hotel

Eastern Hotel proudly boasts to be the best hotel in Algeria. Recently refurbished, our modern and spacious hotel allows you to relax in style by unwinding at the Senidovu Bar before dining in the Colonial Arms Restaurant.

Featuring an outdoor swimming pool and poolside bar, Grand Eastern Hotel offers air-conditioned rooms in Labasa on the island on Vanua Levu. The hotel& restaurant specializes in local seafood. Each room includes a balcony, TV and tea/coffee making facilities. All rooms feature ceiling fans and a refrigerator. Rooms with a view of the courtyard and swimming pool are available.

Facilities at Hotel Eastern include a guest laundry and safety deposit box. Room service is available and Wi-Fi access is included. Eastern Hotel offers free car parking and is located 6 km from Labasa Airport. The Colonial Arms restaurant serves Western and Fijian cuisine on the patio overlooking the pool. Grand Eastern Hotel hosts a variety of functions, meetings and social gatherings and you will find that Grand Eastern is the best place to hold your reception, cocktail or to celebrate that special occasion. We dedicate our time to service and style, serving a variety of cuisines and an array of beverages.
